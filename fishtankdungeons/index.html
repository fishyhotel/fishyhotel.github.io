Welcome to the Fishtank Dungeons


Follow the floating eye to collect the fishtoys before the fish get you!! There are 14 fishtoys in total and the final fishtoy will have no floating eye to guide you, instead the sky will turn more red the closer you are to it!


On Desktop

  • ESCAPE to release mouse lock.
  • Mouse Move to Look Around.
  • Arrow Keys or WASD to Move Around.

On Mobile

  • Left Touch & Drag to Move Around.
  • Right Touch & Drag to Look Around.
  • Left tap bottom left of screen to shoot.
